Find lost HealthCare.gov policies

Surprise! HealthCare.gov randomly strips agent info from policies. No one knows how, or why. It just happens. As far as the selling agent is concerned, the policy, and commission, is lost. Finding and getting paid for those lost polices is extremely difficult, and most agents and agencies will not even try. CYA can help. It will keep accurate records of every policy sold, and, allow agents to easily check-off policies as they show up on a commission statement. With a couple clicks, agents will be able to see which policies may have been lost. A couple more clicks will create the proof insurance companies need to investigate and make it right.
