CAPTURE YOUR APP (CYA)

With just one click, Capture Your Apps allows you to import online insurance applications directly into our database as you create them. Agents, and agencies alike, will save the time and aggravation of application data entry. Additionally, since CYA provides the exact information sent to the carrier, typos and data inconsistencies will be a thing of the past.

Easy to use

Download and install CYA from your RS Agency Services Back Office.
Launch CYA on your computer. It will ask you to login to ensure your apps get safely and securely to your online account.
Then use the built-in internet browser to create your apps as you normally do.
CYA will tell you which pages to Capture. All you’ll have to do is click the Capture button one time for each page. It’s just that easy!

Ideal for agents

Let’s face it.

Most agents struggle with record keeping.

CYA is the perfect tool for the self-employed agent.
CYA accurately records sales as they are made. Plus, the Back Office provides an easy way to keep track of which policies have been paid.
More importantly, it shows which policies have NOT been paid AND provides the proof insurance companies will need to make it right.

Ideal for agencies

CYA is the perfect tool to streamline the collection of submitted application information from all your agents. Data entry time is eliminated freeing up admin resources for more important activities. Accurate carrier, product and premium information is captured every time eliminating data entry inconsistencies. Additionally, CYA tracks and stores the name and ID of the agent taking the app. This is invaluable tracking information for agencies that submit all their business under one writing number.

Get more out of Open Enrollment!

CYA can save several hours a week during Open Enrollment. No more stopping to record policies sold. Just keep on selling!

Find lost HealthCare.gov policies

Surprise! HealthCare.gov randomly strips agent info from policies. No one knows how, or why. It just happens. As far as the selling agent is concerned, the policy, and commission, is lost. Finding and getting paid for those lost polices is extremely difficult, and most agents and agencies will not even try. CYA can help. It will keep accurate records of every policy sold, and, allow agents to easily check-off policies as they show up on a commission statement. With a couple clicks, agents will be able to see which policies may have been lost. A couple more clicks will create the proof insurance companies need to investigate and make it right.

Get Captured Apps into Your CRM

If you have an account with a supported Client Relationship Manager (CRM) company, your captured apps can be sent directly to your CRM as they are captured. If your CRM is not supported yet, your captured apps can be exported to a CSV file which can be easily imported into any CRM.
